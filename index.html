Denpasar

Denpasar is the capital city of Bali. The main activities here would be eating Indonesian food, seeing temples, going to markets and beaches.

Some popular places to visit :

  • Bajra Sandhi Monument
  • Bali Museum
  • Sanur Beach
  • Art Centre

Badung

Badung is one of regencies in Bali. Badung has a lot of tourist destination, the most popular destination are Kuta beach and Garuda Wisnu Kencana as a 122-meter tall statue in Badung, Bali.

Some popular places to visit :

  • Garuda Wisnu Kencana (GWK) statue
  • Kuta Beach
  • Canggu Beach
  • Uluwatu Temple

Nusa Penida

Nusa Penida island is a small paradise located at the East Coast of Bali. Nusa Penida island has amazing underwater views for snorkeling and diving.

Some popular places to visit :

  • Broken Beach
  • Angel's Billabong
  • Kelingking Beach
  • Crystal Bay

Nusa Lembongan

Nusa Lembongan island is a small island off the Southeast Coast of Bali. Just like Nusa Penida, Nusa Lembongan also has wonderful underwater views for snorkeling and diving. Surfing is also a fun thing to do because of the high waves on the coast.

Some popular places to visit :

  • Devil's Tear
  • Blue Lagoon
  • Lembongan Mangrove Forest
  • Yellow Bridge
